Healthy Kuwait - Reduce Sugar

Less sugar. Healthier families. A stronger Kuwait.

Healthy Kuwait is a 12-month public awareness campaign focused on reducing sugar consumption among youth and families through education, social media outreach, workshops, and community health events.

The Problem

Too much sugar is feeding a national health crisis.

Kuwait ranks among the countries with high diabetes prevalence. Excess sugar intake contributes to major risks including type 2 diabetes, heart disease, and high blood pressure.

Why this matters now

The campaign focuses on prevention through awareness, behavior change, and community participation instead of waiting for health problems to escalate. Because apparently humans do enjoy making simple things medically catastrophic.

Type 2 Diabetes

Linked to long-term excess sugar intake and poor lifestyle habits.

Heart Disease

Higher sugar consumption can increase long-term cardiovascular risk.

High Blood Pressure

Excess sugar can affect weight, metabolism, and blood pressure outcomes.
